Win Themes

4 Positioning Pillars

01
Compliance-First Architecture
Lead with DLT consent management. Banks buy CPaaS to grow — they switch CPaaS to stay compliant. Owning the compliance narrative wins the CTO before the commercial conversation starts.
02
Vendor Consolidation ROI
The average BFSI company has 3.2 CPaaS vendors. Consolidating to one reduces licensing cost by 20–30%, cuts integration maintenance, and eliminates multi-vendor SLA blame games.
03
Personalised Video at Scale
VSPAGY personalised video for loan statements and policy renewals drives 3–4x engagement vs. static SMS. Differentiated capability that no competitor offers natively in India.
04
India-Right Pricing Model
Per-message pricing built for Indian transaction volumes — not global CPaaS rates. Combined with on-premise deployment option for RBI data localisation compliance.
