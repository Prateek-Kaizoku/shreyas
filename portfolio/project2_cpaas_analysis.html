Strategic Analysis

4 Win Themes for the BFSI Segment

01

Compliance as a Moat

Every Indian bank is under TRAI scrutiny for DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology) compliance. The CPaaS vendor with the deepest DLT and consent management layer wins the risk-averse CISO conversation — before price is even discussed.

02

Engagement Beyond Delivery

SMS OTP and push notifications are solved problems. The next battleground is engagement after delivery — personalised video statements, interactive loan summaries, and AI-driven upsell triggers embedded in transactional messages.

03

India-Optimised Pricing

Global CPaaS players (Infobip, Twilio) price for global markets. India BFSI deals require competitive per-message pricing plus an on-premise or hybrid deployment option for data localisation. Local players who match this model win on TCO.

04

Omnichannel or Single-Vendor Lock

Banks that standardise on a single CPaaS vendor for SMS, WhatsApp, voice, email, and video gain negotiating leverage and operational simplicity. The vendor who can credibly own all five channels wins the multi-year contract — not just the pilot.
