Segment Messaging

Opening Message by Vertical

BFSI
The Compliance Hook
"Every bank using >2 CPaaS vendors is one TRAI audit away from a compliance gap they didn't build. I'd like 20 minutes to show you how [Bank] can consolidate before the 2026 deadline."
Lead with the regulatory urgency — then the consolidation ROI. BFSI buyers are risk-averse. The compliance angle gets the discovery call. The ROI closes it.
IT / ITES
The Developer-First Hook
"Your team is using [vendor] for SMS and a separate provider for WhatsApp. I've seen 40+ IT companies fix this with a single API layer — one SDK, one dashboard, one SLA. Worth a look?"
Tech teams respond to simplification. Show the unified API, the sandbox, the docs. Get engineering to pull in procurement — not the other way around.
Manufacturing
The Dealer Network Hook
"[Company] has 200+ dealers sending updates to customers via personal WhatsApp. That's not a communication strategy — it's a compliance liability. Here's how to fix it in 60 days."
Manufacturing CXOs care about brand consistency and dealer compliance. The DPDP Act (2023) makes personal WhatsApp use for business comms a legal risk. Own that narrative.
Healthcare
The Patient Experience Hook
"85% of missed appointments at Indian hospitals happen because the reminder SMS either didn't arrive or went to spam. I can show you how Max Healthcare dropped missed appointments by 34%."
Healthcare buyers respond to patient outcomes and operational cost. Lead with the missed appointment data — then show the WhatsApp appointment confirmation flow. Concreteness wins.
