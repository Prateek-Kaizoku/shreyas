Health Score Dashboard

5-Factor Health Score Model

Factor 1 · Weight 30%
Product Usage
Monthly active usage vs. contracted volume. Below 60% of contracted messages = amber. Below 40% = red. Triggers proactive check-in.
30%
Factor 2 · Weight 25%
Support Ticket Volume
P1/P2 ticket count in last 30 days. Any unresolved P1 > 48 hours = immediate escalation. P2 > 5 in a month = amber health signal.
25%
Factor 3 · Weight 20%
Stakeholder Engagement
Meeting attendance, QBR participation, and response rate from economic buyer. Single-threaded accounts (only one contact) are high churn risk.
20%
Factor 4 · Weight 15%
Feature Adoption Breadth
Number of distinct features/channels in active use. Accounts using 4+ features have 2.4x higher renewal rates than single-feature accounts.
15%
Factor 5 · Weight 10%
NPS & Sentiment
Quarterly NPS pulse. Detractors (0–6) trigger immediate CS outreach. Also monitors Slack sentiment and QBR feedback for early warning signals.
10%

Amber (50–74): Weekly CS touchpoint

Upsell Framework

Expansion Triggers & Actions

Trigger 1
Volume Approaching Contract Cap
When account hits 80% of contracted message volume before month-end, proactively offer volume upgrade with a 3-year lock-in discount. Prevents billing surprise and creates expansion revenue.
→ Offer: Volume tier upgrade + 3-year pricing lock
Trigger 2
New Team or Department Signal
LinkedIn or internal org chart changes showing new team using communications tools. Reach out with a "new team onboarding" offer — get a second business unit live before the competitor does.
→ Offer: Multi-team package with onboarding included
Trigger 3
Single-Channel to Multi-Channel
Accounts live only on SMS who haven't activated WhatsApp, voice, or video. Show them WhatsApp engagement rates vs. SMS. The data sells itself — 40%+ open rate vs. 8–10%.
→ Offer: WhatsApp Business API pilot at no additional cost for 30 days
Trigger 4
Funding / M&A Event
Series B/C raises, acquisitions, or market expansion news. New funding means new budget cycle and new communication needs. Strike within 2 weeks of announcement.
→ Offer: Enterprise plan with dedicated CSM + SLA upgrade
