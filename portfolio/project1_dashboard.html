Design Decisions & Architecture

📊
Data Architecture
All KPI data stored in JavaScript objects — monthly arrays for units, market share, VAS penetration, and digital adoption — mirroring how a real territory manager tracks performance in a CRM or BI tool. Quarter filtering dynamically slices the same dataset.
Structured Data
🔄
Interactivity
Quarter filter buttons dynamically update all KPI headlines and re-render Chart.js charts from the same underlying dataset. This mirrors real BI tools like Looker or Power BI where a single filter propagates across the entire report view.

Visual Design
Dark theme mirrors professional analytics platforms (Looker, Tableau, Power BI). Gold accent matches Tata Motors' brand tone. Animated progress bars and smooth chart transitions improve readability during management presentations.

Outcomes

What the Numbers Actually Mean

#1

National VAS Penetration — Tata Motors

Ranked #1 across all Tata Motors territories nationally for VAS (Value-Added Services) penetration. This metric tracks attach rates on insurance, finance, and extended warranty products. The dashboard made it possible to monitor and act on the data in real time.

21%

Lead Conversion Improvement via Digital Adoption

By tracking digital tool adoption alongside conversion rates, the dashboard revealed a direct correlation between CRM usage and close rates. This insight drove targeted coaching at the two underperforming dealerships.

3%

Year-on-Year Market Share Growth

Haryana territory grew from 28% to 31% market share over the tracked period. The monthly drill-down in the dashboard identified which months and which dealers were driving or dragging the average — enabling targeted interventions.

3

Dealerships Monitored — Single View

Previously, each dealership reported separately in Excel. This dashboard unified all three into one filterable view — reducing monthly reporting time by ~3 hours and giving management a consistent format for every review call.
